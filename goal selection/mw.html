Poha

Instructions:

Ingredients:
- 1 cup flattened rice (poha)
- 1 tbsp oil
- 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
- 1 small onion, chopped
- 1 green chili, chopped
- 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
- Salt to taste
- Fresh coriander leaves, chopped
- Lemon wedges for garnish
Steps:
1. Rinse poha in water and drain well.
2. Heat oil in a pan, add mustard seeds and let them splutter.
3. Add onions and green chili, and sauté until onions are soft.
4. Add turmeric powder, salt, and poha, and mix well.
5. Cook for a few minutes until poha is heated through.
6. Garnish with coriander leaves and lemon wedges before serving.

Upma

Instructions:

Ingredients:
- 1 cup semolina (suji)
- 2 tbsp oil
- 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
- 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
- 1 small onion, chopped
- 1/2 cup mixed vegetables (peas, carrots, beans)
- 2 cups water
- Salt to taste
Steps:
1. Heat oil in a pan, add mustard and cumin seeds.
2. Add onions and sauté until translucent.
3. Add vegetables and sauté for a few minutes.
4. Add water and salt, bring it to a boil.
5. Slowly add semolina (suji) while stirring to avoid lumps.
6. Cover and cook for 2-3 minutes until the water is absorbed.
7. Serve hot with coconut chutney.

Khichdi

Instructions:

Ingredients:
- 1/2 cup rice
- 1/2 cup split green gram (moong dal)
- 4 cups water
- Salt to taste
- 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
- 1 tbsp ghee or oil
- 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
- Optional: Chopped vegetables (carrot, peas)
Steps:
1. Rinse rice and dal well, and soak for 10 minutes.
2. In a pot, add rice, dal, water, salt, and turmeric, and bring to a boil.
3. Simmer until rice and dal are soft and cooked.
4. In a separate pan, heat ghee, add cumin seeds and optional vegetables.
5. Pour this tempering over the cooked khichdi and mix well.
6. Serve hot, optionally garnish with coriander leaves.
