Dalia

Instructions:

Ingredients:
- 1 cup Dalia (broken wheat)
- 2 cups water or milk
- Salt to taste
- 1 tbsp ghee (clarified butter)
- 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
- 1/4 tsp turmeric
- Fresh vegetables like carrots, peas (optional)
Steps:
1. In a pan, heat ghee and add cumin seeds.
2. Add vegetables and sauté until soft.
3. Add water or milk and bring it to a boil.
4. Add dalia, salt, and turmeric.
5. Cook until dalia is soft and water is absorbed.
6. Serve hot, optionally garnish with coriander leaves.

Upma

Instructions:

Ingredients:
- 1 cup semolina (suji)
- 2 tbsp oil
- 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
- 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
- 1 small onion, chopped
- 1/2 cup mixed vegetables (peas, carrots, beans)
- 2 cups water
- Salt to taste
Steps:
1. Heat oil in a pan, add mustard and cumin seeds.
2. Add onions and sauté until translucent.
3. Add vegetables and sauté for a few minutes.
4. Add water and salt, bring it to a boil.
5. Slowly add semolina (suji) while stirring to avoid lumps.
6. Cover and cook for 2-3 minutes until the water is absorbed.
7. Serve hot with coconut chutney.

Cucumber Detox Water

Instructions:

Ingredients:
- 1 cucumber, thinly sliced
- 1 lemon, sliced
- Fresh mint leaves
- 4 cups water
- Ice cubes (optional)
Steps:
1. Wash the cucumber, lemon, and mint leaves thoroughly.
2. Slice the cucumber and lemon.
3. In a pitcher, add cucumber slices, lemon slices, and mint leaves.
4. Fill with water and let it infuse for 1-2 hours in the refrigerator.
5. Serve chilled with ice cubes.
