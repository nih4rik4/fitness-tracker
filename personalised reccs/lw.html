Dal

Instructions:

Ingredients:
- 1 cup lentils (moong or toor dal)
- 3 cups water
- Salt to taste
- 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
- 1 tbsp ghee or oil
- 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
- 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
- 1 small tomato, chopped (optional)
Steps:
1. Rinse the lentils well and soak for 10 minutes.
2. In a pot, add lentils, water, salt, and turmeric, and bring to a boil.
3. Simmer until the lentils are soft.
4. In a separate pan, heat ghee, add cumin and mustard seeds, then add tomatoes.
5. Pour this tempering over the cooked dal and mix well.
6. Serve hot with rice or chapati.

Besan Chilla

Instructions:

Ingredients:
- 1 cup gram flour (besan)
- 1/4 cup water
- Salt to taste
- 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
- 1/4 tsp red chili powder
- 1 small onion, finely chopped
- 1 green chili, chopped (optional)
- 1 tbsp chopped coriander leaves
Steps:
1. In a bowl, mix besan, water, salt, turmeric, and chili powder to form a batter.
2. Add chopped onion, green chili, and coriander leaves.
3. Heat a pan with a little oil and pour a ladleful of the batter, spreading it thin.
4. Cook until golden on both sides, about 2-3 minutes each side.
5. Serve hot with chutney or yogurt.

Tea with Less Sugar

Instructions:

Ingredients:
- 1 cup water
- 1/2 tsp tea leaves or tea bag
- 1/4 tsp ginger, grated (optional)
- 1-2 tsp sugar (adjust as desired)
- 1/4 cup milk
Steps:
1. In a pot, bring water and ginger to a boil.
2. Add tea leaves and sugar, let it simmer for 2-3 minutes.
3. Add milk and bring it to a boil again.
4. Strain and serve hot.
5. Adjust sugar to taste.
