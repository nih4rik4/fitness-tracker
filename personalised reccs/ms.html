Ashwagandha Infused Modak

Instructions:

Ingredients:
- 1 cup rice flour
- 1 cup water
- 1/2 cup grated coconut
- 1/2 cup jaggery, grated
- 1 tsp ashwagandha powder
- 1/4 tsp cardamom powder
- 1 tsp ghee
Steps:
1. Heat a pan and add grated coconut, jaggery, and ashwagandha powder.
2. Cook until jaggery melts and mixture thickens. Add cardamom powder and set aside.
3. In a separate pot, boil water, add a pinch of salt, and then add rice flour.
4. Mix well and let it cool. Knead into a smooth dough.
5. Take small portions of dough, make a small cup shape, and fill with coconut mixture.
6. Seal and shape into modaks. Steam for 10-15 minutes and serve warm.

Chaas (Buttermilk)

Instructions:

Ingredients:
- 1 cup yogurt
- 2 cups water
- Salt to taste
- 1/2 tsp roasted cumin powder
- Fresh mint or coriander leaves
- 1 green chili, chopped (optional)
Steps:
1. In a blender, add yogurt, water, salt, and cumin powder.
2. Blend until smooth and frothy.
3. Pour into glasses, add chopped mint or coriander for garnish.
4. Serve chilled, optionally with green chili for extra spice.

Khichdi

Instructions:

Ingredients:
- 1/2 cup rice
- 1/2 cup split green gram (moong dal)
- 4 cups water
- Salt to taste
- 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
- 1 tbsp ghee or oil
- 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
- Optional: Chopped vegetables (carrot, peas)
Steps:
1. Rinse rice and dal well, and soak for 10 minutes.
2. In a pot, add rice, dal, water, salt, and turmeric, and bring to a boil.
3. Simmer until rice and dal are soft and cooked.
4. In a separate pan, heat ghee, add cumin seeds and optional vegetables.
5. Pour this tempering over the cooked khichdi and mix well.
6. Serve hot, optionally garnish with coriander leaves.
